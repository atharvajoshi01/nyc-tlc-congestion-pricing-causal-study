Average Fare by Group

Mean fare per metered trip. The added $0.74 CBD fee post-Jan-5-2025 is too small to move demand visibly.

Treatment Group

Yellow / green taxi trips with pickup or dropoff inside the Central Business District (south of 60th St, Manhattan).

Control Group

Trips entirely within Upper Manhattan, outside the CBD zone. Used to control for citywide trends.

Method

Difference-in-differences regression with day-of-week and month fixed effects, clustered standard errors. DuckDB + statsmodels on 95M+ TLC records.
